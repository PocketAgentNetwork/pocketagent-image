# BOOTSTRAP.md - First-Run Ritual

## What This File Does
One-time setup ritual for a brand-new PocketAgent workspace.

## First-Run Setup

Welcome! This is your first time running PocketAgent.

### Step 1: Fill in your identity
- Open IDENTITY.md and add your agent's name and emoji

### Step 2: Tell us about yourself
- Open USER.md and fill in your information

### Step 3: Define your agent's job
- Open JOB.md and describe what you want your agent to do

### Step 4: Review the soul
- Read SOUL.md to understand your agent's personality
- Customize if needed

### Step 5: Set up tools (optional)
- Open TOOLS.md and document your local environment

### Step 6: You're ready!
- Your PocketAgent is now configured
- Delete this BOOTSTRAP.md file
- Start using your agent!
